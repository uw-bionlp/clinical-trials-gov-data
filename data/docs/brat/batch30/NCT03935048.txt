Inclusion Criteria:
  -  Resides in Northwest Arkansas
  -  Age 18+ years
  -  Metabolic Syndrome (characterized by participant having three or more of the following measurements: abdominal obesity, triglyceride level over 150 mg/dl, HDL cholesterol <
     40 mg/dl in men and 50 mg/dl in women, systolic blood pressure of 130 mm Hg or diastolic blood pressure of 85 mm Hg, and/or fasting glucose > 100 mg/dL)
  -  All ethnicities
  -  Female and male
  -  Currently consuming a high glycemic load diet
Exclusion Criteria:
  -  Food allergies
  -  Dietary restrictions (e.g. vegetarian, vegan, etc.)
  -  Trying to lose weight in last 3 months
  -  Prescription medications related to heart disease or type 2 diabetes
  -  Fear of needles
