Inclusion Criteria:
PSA level 2,5-10mg/dl Approving the transrectal prostate biopsy and mpMR NO
contraindication for MR: glomerular filtration rate (GFR) >60ml/dk, no claustrophobia, no
prosthesis or cardiac pacemaker which is not suitable for MR no contraindication for
stopping antiaggregant or antithrombotic treatment. no history of previous prostate biopsy
no diagnosis of prostate cancer or other malignancies
Exclusion Criteria:
  -  not approving the biopsy and MR imaging
  -  having prosthesis or cardiac pace maker not suitable for MR
  -  having contraindication for stopping antiaggregant or antithrombotic treatment.
  -  history of previous prostate biopsy
