Inclusion Criteria : 
  -  All patients older than  18 years undergoing elective surgery that receive a pre operative visit by anesthesiologists . 
Exclusion Criteria : 
  -  Patients undergoing emergency surgery
       -  Patients under  18 years of age
       -  Patients who refuse to participate
       -  Patients unable to perform the  6 MWT
       -  Claudication of the lower extremities and inability to maintain a constant pace of walking on level ground
