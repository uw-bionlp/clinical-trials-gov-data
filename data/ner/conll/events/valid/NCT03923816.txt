Exclusion Criteria : 
  -   1 - patient refusal .   2 - psychiatric disorders .   3 - pregnancy and lactation .   4 - preexisting neurological dysfunction ( history of cerebrovascular stroke CVS )  5 - metastatic cancer . 
      6 - Inflammatory bowel disease .   7 - Diabetes mellitus .   8 - Renal insufficiency ( serum creatinine level more than  180 Î¼mol / l )  .   9 - unexpected intraoperative findings ( inoperable )  .   10 - accidental massive intraoperative hemorrhage .
