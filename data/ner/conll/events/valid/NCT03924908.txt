Inclusion Criteria : 
  -  Burn patient treated as outpatient
  -  Burn surface area > or = 5 % of the total body surface area
  -  mainly 2 nd degree burn
Exclusion Criteria : 
  -  Psychiatric antecedents
  -  Claustrophobia
  -  Heavy hearing
  -  Visual impairment
  -  Face burn
  -  Conjunctivitis
  -  Consent not obtained
