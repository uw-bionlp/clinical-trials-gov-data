Inclusion Criteria : 
  -  Nulliparous singleton pregnancy
  -  gestational age between  37 week and  42 weeks
  -  Cephalic presentation
  -  Bishop score of  5 or less
  -  No clinical evidence of regular contraction
Exclusion Criteria : 
  -  Rupture of membrane
  -  Vaginal bleeding
  -  Any contraindication to vaginal delivery
