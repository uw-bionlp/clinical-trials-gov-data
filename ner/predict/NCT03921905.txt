Inclusion Criteria :  -  Patients of both sexes with a diagnosis of stable CAD as follows : acute myocardial infarction or percutaneous coronary revascularization or coronary artery bypass graft surgery occured at least 12 months before entering the study or chronic stable angina .  Exclusion Criteria :  -  Mental illness limiting the capacity of self - care or any condition limiting seriously the life expectancy less than 12 months .
