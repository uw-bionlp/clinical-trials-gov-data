Inclusion Criteria:
  -  At least 18 years of age.
  -  Enrollment in the UAB CMC-ADHF study under the separate UAB approved research protocol (IRB-300000114, PI Rajapreyar)
  -  Negative urine or serum Î²-hCG test within 48 hours of [11C] acetate administration in women of child bearing potential. Women who are post-menopausal with at least 1 year since last menses or documented surgical sterilization will not require pregnancy testing.
Exclusion Criteria:
  -  Inability to provide informed consent
  -  Pregnancy
  -  Inability to lie still for the imaging study
  -  Weight exceeding the weight limit of the PET imaging table (500 pounds).
