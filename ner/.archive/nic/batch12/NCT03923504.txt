Inclusion Criteria:
  -  Ability to read and understand and willing to sign a written informed consent form.
  -  Diagnosis of stage I-IV non- or Hodgkin lymphoma and expect to receive an anthracycline based chemotherapeutic regimen.
  -  Capacity to walk at least 2 city blocks on a flat surface
Exclusion Criteria:
  -  Contraindications for MRI
  -  Contraindications for exercise training or testing
  -  Active Pregnancy
  -  Subjects with claustrophobia
  -  Inability to provide informed consent
