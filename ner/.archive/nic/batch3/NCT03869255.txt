For community patients :
Inclusion Criteria:
  -  the selection criteria for blood donation
  -  age ≥ 18 years old
  -  No refusal of the person
Exclusion Criteria:
  -  None
For hospitalized patients :
Inclusion Criteria:
  -  age ≥ 18 years old
  -  hospitalization in one of the services participating in the study
  -  no refusal of the person
Exclusion Criteria:
  -  None
