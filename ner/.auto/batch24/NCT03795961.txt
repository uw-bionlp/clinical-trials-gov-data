Inclusion Criteria:
Patient diagnosed as CTS by history, clinical examination and NCS for more than 3 months.
Both genders. Adult of aged 18 years and above. Able to understand the informed consent.
Exclusion Criteria:
Patients with diabetes mellitus, collagen disorders, thyroid disease, peripheral
neuropathy, traumatic nerve injury, cervical radiculopathy, fibromyalgia.
Pregnancy. Malignant. Patients with any clinically significant or unstable medical or
psychiatric disorder.
