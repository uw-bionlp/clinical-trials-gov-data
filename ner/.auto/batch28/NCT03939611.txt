Inclusion Criteria:
Infants were included who were aged 1-3 months, Infants who were diagnosed IC by a
pediatrician according to Wessel's rule of threes.
Exclusion Criteria (Beckmann & Le Quesne, 2005):
Infants who were using any of analgesic drug until 3 hours before the applications and
antibiotic or steroid due to treating an illness, Infants who had an acute fever,
musculoskeletal disease, active shingles or infection, acute shingles, or tissue in the
foot and joint, All infants with any non-IC health problems.
