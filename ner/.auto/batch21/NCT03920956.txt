Inclusion Criteria:
  -  Adults greater than or equal to 18 years of age suspected by their physician of having
     "white coat" or "masked" hypertension (HTN), hypotension symptoms due to antihypertensive therapy, uncontrolled sustained HTN, requiring additional confirmation of an initial diagnosis if HTN, or resistant HTN.
Exclusion Criteria:
  -  Documented past medical history of persistent atrial fibrillation, on hemodialysis, pregnancy, or dementia.
