Inclusion Criteria:
Patients with any incipient non cavitated posterior and anterior proximal caries.
Patient is 7-10 mixed dentition ICDAS Score 1 or 2
Exclusion Criteria:
Any patients with any serious chronic medical problem, caries that cross the DEJ
(dentin-enamel junction), enamel discontinuity, history of proximal restorations, resin
infiltrate and ICDAS score of proximal caries more than two clinically or radiographically.
