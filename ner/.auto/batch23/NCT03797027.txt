Inclusion Criteria:
  -  Willing to receive PCNL
  -  Aged 18-70 years
  -  Normal renal function
  -  ASA scoreⅠ to Ⅱ
Exclusion Criteria:
  -  Uncorrected coagulopathy and active urinary tract infection (UTI)
  -  Patients who underwent transplant or urinary diversion.
  -  Congenital abnormalities.
