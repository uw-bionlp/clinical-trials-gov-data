Inclusion Criteria:

  6. Comprehension of English language
  7. Authorized legal representative able and willing to give informed consent

Exclusion Criteria:

  2. Diagnosis of Non-AD Dementia
  3. Lifetime or current diagnosis of Schizophrenia, Bipolar Disorder or Schizoaffective Disorder
  4. Current episode of Major Depressive Disorder (MDD)
  5. Active substance use disorder within past 6 months
  6. Treatment with ECT or other neurostimulation therapies (e.g., TMS or vagal nerve stimulation) within the past 3 months
