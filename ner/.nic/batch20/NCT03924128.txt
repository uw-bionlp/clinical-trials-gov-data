Inclusion Criteria:
  -  Selected will be subjects to meeting the following criteria: male and female subjects age ≥ 20 years old, diagnosed with Osteoarthritis stages III and IV according to the American College of Rheumatology, manifesting pain, functional impairment for more than 3 months, able to wander with or without canes.
Exclusion Criteria:
  -  Will be excluded from study subjects with history of cancer, unstable cardiovascular disease, uncontrolled hypertension, morbid obesity, that is, BMI ≥ 40, terminal illness, cerebrovascular disease, dementia, heart pacemaker, superior or inferior limb fractures.
